About Me

My name is Anthony Michel and I am a full-stack web developer! I received my qualification from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill after successfully completing Coding Bootcamp.

Prior to becoming a web developer, I obtained my Bachelor of Nursing degree as well as an Associate Degree in Policing Practice; having then worked for 7 years as a Perioperative Registered Nurse (as a Scrub, Scout, Anaesthetics and in Recovery) and 5 years as a Patrol Officer with the New South Wales Police Force - back in my native home of Australia!

In my spare time I enjoy exploring the outdoors here in Western North Carolina as well as being an avid video-game enthusiast!
